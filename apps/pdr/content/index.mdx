## Strategic Alignment

This initiative directly supports three pillars of the company's FY26 strategy:

1. **Data-Driven Decision Making** — Empower all teams with self-service analytics
2. **Platform Consolidation** — Reduce tool sprawl from 6 systems to 2
3. **Developer Productivity** — Improve internal tooling velocity by 40%
