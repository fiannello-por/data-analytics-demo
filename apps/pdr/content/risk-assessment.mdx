## Risk Matrix

| ID  | Risk                            | Probability | Impact   | Score | Status     |
| --- | ------------------------------- | ----------- | -------- | ----- | ---------- |
| R1  | Snowflake costs exceed budget   | Medium      | High     | 🟠    | Mitigating |
| R2  | Team lacks Lightdash expertise  | High        | Medium   | 🟠    | Mitigating |
| R3  | Data migration causes downtime  | Low         | Critical | 🟡    | Monitoring |
| R4  | Stakeholder adoption is low     | Medium      | High     | 🟠    | Mitigating |
| R5  | EOL tools fail before migration | Low         | Critical | 🟡    | Monitoring |

<Callout type="warn">
  **R1 and R4 are the highest-priority risks.** Both have active mitigation
  plans in progress. See details below.
</Callout>

## Mitigation Plans

### R1: Snowflake Cost Overrun

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Set Up Cost Monitoring

    Deploy Snowflake resource monitors with automatic suspension at 80% of monthly budget threshold.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Implement Query Governance

    Require all production queries to use tagged warehouses with per-team cost allocation.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Optimize Incrementally

    Convert full-refresh dbt models to incremental models, targeting 60% compute reduction.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Monthly Review Cadence

    Finance and Data Eng review actual vs. projected spend monthly, with escalation path to VP.

  </Step>
</Steps>

### R2: Team Capability Gap

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Training Program

    Two-week intensive Lightdash + dbt training for all 8 engineers before Phase 1 begins.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Embed Expert

    Contract a Lightdash implementation specialist for the first 3 months.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Knowledge Base

    Build internal runbooks and FAQs during Phase 1, reviewed by the embedded expert.

  </Step>
</Steps>

### R4: Low Stakeholder Adoption

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Early Champions Program

    Identify 5 power users from Sales and Marketing to beta-test during Phase 1.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Training Workshops

    Run bi-weekly hands-on workshops for business users during Phase 2 rollout.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Feedback Loop

    Weekly office hours + dedicated Slack channel for questions and feature requests.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Deprecation Timeline

    Announce clear sunset dates for legacy tools to drive migration urgency.

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warn">
  **Contingency**: If adoption remains below 40% after 90 days, we will conduct
  a formal retrospective and may pivot the UX approach for Phase 3.
</Callout>
